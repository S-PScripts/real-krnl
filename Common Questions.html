Krnl Common Questions

I can’t search scripts in the script hub.

That’s not Krnl’s fault — it’s Scriptblox. Use a VPN such as ProtonVPN or Cloudflare WARP to bypass the rate limit. If it happens again, switch VPN servers or try another VPN.

Why can’t I buy things in Roblox with Krnl?

Krnl Roblox has an untoggleable anti-buy for safety. If you want to spend Robux, install the normal Roblox app instead.

Krnl crashes when I open it.

Make sure you open Roblox in portrait mode (keep auto-rotate OFF). If it still crashes, try opening in landscape too. You may also need to adjust your DPI and restart your phone to fix it.

I keep getting kicked from Roblox games.

This might be caused by something in your auto-execute. Use Zarchiver or CX File Explorer, go to the Krnl folder, and delete everything inside the autoexec folder. You might need to reinstall Krnl afterwards.
